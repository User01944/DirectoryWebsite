About Trip to Bhutan - Bhutan Dew Drop Travel

Trip to Bhutan - Bhutan Dew Drop Travel is a licensed tour operator based in Thimphu, Bhutan. This company operates under the auspices of the Royal Government of Bhutan and specializes in providing unique cultural and adventure travel experiences. By leveraging a deep understanding of Bhutan's history, legends, and customs, Bhutan Dew Drop Travel aims to create memorable journeys for visitors exploring the rich tapestry of this Himalayan kingdom.


Location of Bhutan Dew Drop Travel

Located at Deki House in Babesa, Thimphu, Bhutan Dew Drop Travel is strategically positioned to offer travelers easy access to some of the most breathtaking landscapes and cultural sites in the region. The company prides itself on its extensive knowledge of the local flora, fauna, and the various adventure treks that Bhutan has to offer. With a focus on delivering authentic experiences, Bhutan Dew Drop Travel is an excellent choice for those looking to immerse themselves in Bhutanese culture.


What Services Does Bhutan Dew Drop Travel Offer?

Bhutan Dew Drop Travel offers a diverse range of services tailored to meet the needs of various travelers. The company specializes in cultural tours that delve into the heart of Bhutanese traditions, allowing visitors to experience local festivals, visit ancient monasteries, and engage with the vibrant communities that call Bhutan home. Additionally, adventure travel options include trekking, hiking, and exploring the stunning natural landscapes that Bhutan is famous for.


The knowledgeable guides at Bhutan Dew Drop Travel enhance the travel experience by sharing insights into the historical significance of various sites and the ecological diversity of the region. This commitment to providing in-depth cultural and adventure travel sets Bhutan Dew Drop Travel apart from other tour operators, ensuring that every journey is both educational and enjoyable.


Why Choose Bhutan Dew Drop Travel for Your Trip to Bhutan?

Choosing Bhutan Dew Drop Travel for a trip to Bhutan means opting for a company that prioritizes customer satisfaction and authentic experiences. The team is composed of passionate individuals who possess extensive knowledge of the country’s history, culture, and natural environment. This expertise allows them to curate personalized itineraries that cater to individual interests and preferences.


Furthermore, Bhutan Dew Drop Travel operates under the guidelines set by the Royal Government of Bhutan, ensuring that all tours comply with local regulations and sustainability practices. By choosing this company, travelers contribute to the sustainable tourism model that Bhutan is known for.

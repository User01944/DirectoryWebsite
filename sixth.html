About Basnet Attorneys and Law


Basnet Attorneys & Law is a top-rated law firm in Thimphu, Bhutan, providing exceptional legal services to clients across the nation and worldwide. Our team of highly experienced Bhutanese lawyers specializes in litigation, arbitration, legal consulting, and business advisory. With an in-depth understanding of Bhutan's legal landscape, we skillfully navigate clients through the complexities of the country's legal system. Our attorneys are dedicated to delivering tailored solutions that address the unique needs of each client, ensuring favorable outcomes in legal matters both within Bhutan and internationally.


Recognized for our expertise, Basnet Attorneys & Law has successfully collaborated with prominent law firms in India and the United States, as well as reputable intellectual property firms in Germany, India, Singapore, and China, to name a few. Our client base includes major international businesses from India, China, and Switzerland, with head offices spanning over 100 countries and multiple subsidiaries.


Our firm has also provided expert legal advice on Bhutanese matters to international banks and firms worldwide, solidifying our reputation as a trusted authority in the region's legal arena.
